Welcome to Study Buddy

Study Buddy is a web-based educational platform created to help students with homework, concept understanding, and daily study support.

Our goal is to make learning simple, accessible, and stress-free for students, especially school-level learners.

What We Offer

  • Homework assistance and guidance
  • Study support tools for students
  • Easy-to-use and student-friendly interface

Web-Based Service

Study Buddy is primarily offered as an online educational service through this website. Users can access learning support directly from their browser.

Any mobile app versions of Study Buddy, if available, are distributed only through official and trusted app stores. We do not provide direct APK downloads on this website.

Who Can Use Study Buddy?

Study Buddy is suitable for students, learners, and anyone looking for academic support. The content provided is for educational purposes only.
